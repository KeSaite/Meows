Meows

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Meows is a native Android app for monitoring your servers over SSH. This policy explains what it stores and how that information is handled. In short: your data stays on your device, and Meows has no backend of its own.

Information We Store

Everything below is kept locally on your device:

  • Server connection details: SSH host, port, username, and your password or private key (encrypted).
  • Monitoring data: performance metrics (CPU, memory, disk, network, TCP connections) read from the servers you add.
  • Your settings: alert thresholds, notification preferences, and app options you configure.

How the Data Is Used

  • To open SSH connections to the servers you add.
  • To show live status and history charts.
  • To raise notifications when a threshold is crossed or a server goes offline. This is handled entirely on your device — there is no push server.
  • For optional encrypted backup to Google Drive, only with your explicit consent.
  • To show the geographic location of server IP addresses, using an offline database.

Storage & Security

  • Passwords and private keys are encrypted with AES-GCM using keys held in the Android Keystore. An optional master password adds a second layer.
  • All data is stored locally through Android's secure storage.
  • Monitoring data is kept only for a short rolling window on your device, then removed automatically.
  • No monitoring data is sent to any external server.
  • SSH connections go directly from your device to your servers.

Third-Party Services

Meows works on its own. Only two optional integrations can involve a third party:

  • Google Drive API: optional backup and restore, available only after you sign in and grant consent. Your data is encrypted on the device before it is uploaded. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Offline IP database: an IPInfo Lite database bundled inside the app provides IP geolocation. Lookups happen on your device; nothing is sent to any geolocation service.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell, rent or trade your information.
  • We do not share your credentials or monitoring data with anyone.
  • We do not collect analytics, usage statistics, or any device identifier.
  • We do not show advertisements.

Permissions

Meows requests a single runtime permission — notifications — and only so it can alert you. You can deny it and keep using the app; you simply will not receive notification alerts.

You can also turn on an optional privacy mode that hides Meows from the recent-apps screen and blocks screenshots and screen recording.

Your Choices

  • Review your data through the backup/export feature.
  • Delete everything by uninstalling the app.
  • Turn features such as notifications or backup on or off at any time.
  • Choose which servers are monitored.

Data Retention

  • Server configurations are kept until you delete them.
  • Monitoring history is kept only briefly, then cleaned automatically.
  • Everything is permanently removed when you uninstall the app.

Children's Privacy

Meows is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any change will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
